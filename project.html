Why this home?

4436 Derby Place is located 100 m. from Canyon Heights Elementary School, 350 m. from Handsworth Secondary & High School. Edgemont Village, an emblematic place with local cafes, and great shopping and dining are only 1.5 km away.

Moreover, this area has consolidated as one of the highest-value areas for new families, due to the impressive appreciation of its properties during the last few years. Not only are new houses being developed, but most of its houses that were built in the 50’s, are now being remodeled, accelerating its modernization and appreciation.

Recent statistics show it is an active market being one of the top preferences for buyers.

Location
North Vancouver

We chose to start operations in North Vancouver for the following reasons:

  1. Its strategic location with close and easy access to the Downtown area and Sky Grouse, Seymour and Cypress centers.
  2. High quality of life that integrates the incredible Canadian nature and the comforts offered by the city of Vancouver.
  3. It is one of Canada's wealthiest and most beautiful communities.
  4. It is fitted with world-class ski hills, hiking trails, boating and swimming.
  5. One of Metro Vancouver's greenest municipalities, with 11 km of green space.
